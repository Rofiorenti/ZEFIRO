Top view — Hull

Designed for flight

The hull geometry is purpose-built for foiling: a narrow waterplane, optimised centre of gravity, and fully retractable wings that integrate flush into the hull at rest. At speed, Zefiro rises completely above the surface — drag drops by over 80%.

Top view — Interior

8 passengers. Two layouts.

Available in cabin and open-deck versions, the interior seats eight passengers plus driver with generous legroom and unobstructed sightlines. Ergonomics and materials were validated in parallel with the structural design — no compromises.

Side view — Foils retracted

Invisible at rest

With foils fully retracted, Zefiro navigates like a conventional displacement hull — accessible in marinas, canals and shallow waters with no infrastructure changes required. Draft under 0.5 m.

Side view — Foils deployed

Take off.

Above 12 knots the foil system deploys automatically. The hull lifts clear of the water, vibration disappears, noise drops to near zero. Cruising speed of 20 knots on a single charge — 55 nautical miles of range.
